No need for drum notation on paper. Our smartphone application animates the drum notes directly on the drums as falling hit targets.

All you need is a smartphone, Google cardboard, a printer and a drumkit

HOLO beats is compatible with any drums: real or electronic.

HOLO beats is designed with student with various disabilities in mind. We have taken into account students with impaired hearing, vision and reading skills in the design and adapted the exercises to beginners.
